Ralph Barczok is currently data manager at a small family business in Tübingen. He studied Catholic Theology and the Christian Orient in Tübingen (main languages Syriac and Ethiopian). After graduation, he began to write his doctoral thesis on the Vita of Joseph Busnaya at the University of Konstanz under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Dorothea Weltecke. During that time he worked for the Research Centre for Aramaean Studies in Konstanz and later in Frankfurt. He finished his doctoral thesis in 2018.

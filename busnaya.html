  • Vita of Joseph Busnaya

Currently I am working on a interactive digital edition of the Vita of Joseph Busnaya. Since the Syriac text might be interesting to other scholars I publish my results and my experiments here on the fly. Therefore this demo version is subject to constant changes. The text of the reconstruction is not yet finished. Also the variants of the manuscripts have to be checked again.
